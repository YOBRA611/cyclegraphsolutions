Guangzhou Biaobang Car Care Industry Co., Ltd.

Our products are supplied by Guangzhou Biaobang Car Care Industry Co., Ltd., an ISO-9002 certified manufacturer specializing in car care, detailing, and spray paint solutions for the international market.

With over 10 years of industry experience, a highly skilled research and development team, and advanced laboratory support from leading universities, Biaobang consistently delivers innovative and high-performance products.

Their modern factory spans over 80,000 square meters, equipped with 16 fully automated production lines and staffed by over 200 qualified professionals, ensuring efficiency, consistency, and quality at scale.
